Make a Difference

لِّيَشْهَدُوا مَنَافِعَ لَهُمْ وَيَذْكُرُوا اسْمَ اللَّهِ فِي أَيَّامٍ مَّعْلُومَاتٍ عَلَىٰ مَا رَزَقَهُم مِّن بَهِيمَةِ الْأَنْعَامِ ۖ فَكُلُوا مِنْهَا وَأَطْعِمُوا الْبَائِسَ الْفَقِيرَ

"That they may witness benefits for themselves and mention the name of Allah on known days over what He has provided for them of cattle. So eat of them and feed the poor." - Qur'an 22:28

By donating to an Islamic charity for refugees in need of food, you can make a significant impact on their lives. Your contribution not only provides them with a vital source of sustenance but also embodies the Islamic values of compassion, mercy, and generosity. Through your support, you can help alleviate hunger, restore hope, and contribute to building a better future for those who have been displaced. Together, we can fulfill our duty to help those in need and make a positive difference in their lives.
